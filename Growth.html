Kunal Kashikar, the Managing Director of Café Crème with his rich experience of over 15 years in the Food & Beverage industry aims to extend a unique café experience to the Indian youth across the country. The quick growth of Café Crème outlets can be credited to 4 years of Kunal Kashikar’s dedicated efforts at traveling to various places, experiencing and gathering knowledge about revolution in cold coffee segment.

Out of all these efforts in research, was born, Café Crème’s signature drink – KUKA. Open throughout the year, our cafés are serving more than 6 lakh glasses of Kuka every month in 20+ cities in India.

The Indian café industry is expected to be a $410 million industry by 2018. This segment is thus expected to grow rapidly in the coming years. With the Indian youth’s increasing spending power and their modern lifestyle and culture, cafés are increasingly becoming a popular place to frequent.


“With over 40 different varieties of delectable chocolate drinks prepared using the finest quality ingredients, Café Crème presents a promising franchise opportunity for those looking forward to an ever evolving business that’s sure to guarantee you growth and profits.”


Café Crème is made up of a dynamic team of skilled and talented professionals who are passionate about Café Crème’s growth and delivering the unique Kuka experience to as many chocolate fans in the country as we can reach out to. We have been appreciated for our innovative Kuka and fresh fruit fusion drinks by national newspaper, Loksatta and Red FM among other prominent media houses.

Inspired to continue innovating and creating new tastes for our customers to relish, we are constantly involved in Research and Development initiatives to give our customers a great café experience every time.
